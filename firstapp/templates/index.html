Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our chocolates

How should I store my chocolates?

Store in a cool, dry place (16-18°C) away from direct sunlight and strong odors. Do not refrigerate as this can cause bloom.

Do you offer dairy-free or vegan options?

Yes! We have a dedicated range of dark chocolates and vegan truffles made with plant-based ingredients.
